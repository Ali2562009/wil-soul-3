Small observations from outside

On water
"A river doesn't stop moving because rocks are in the way. It finds a new path, or it wears the rock down. Either way, it keeps going."
On trees
"A tree doesn't grow straight because conditions are perfect. It grows toward light, even when the light is far away and the ground is hard."
On silence
"Silence in nature is never empty. It's full of things that don't need words to be real."
On time
"Outside, time moves at the right speed. One hour sitting next to a stream feels longer, and better, than a whole day in a city."
"The natural world has been trying to teach us everything we need to know. We just keep not listening."
— Somewhere, sitting under a tree
