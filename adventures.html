Night · Stars · No plan

Sleeping under stars with zero plan, zero tent, and zero regret

A night that cost nothing and gave me something I still cannot name properly. I lay on my back for three hours watching the Milky Way and thinking: this is free. All of this is completely free.

"The sky was so full of stars it felt unfair. Like God was showing off."

Dawn · Mountains · Tea
Making tea on a mountain at dawn while mist covered everything
Some moments you do not photograph. You understand in real time that capturing them would break them. So you just sit there, holding a warm cup, watching the world wake up.

Water · Alone · Honest
Swimming in a river alone and feeling completely okay about being alive
Cold water is the most honest conversation you can have with yourself. It removes every pretense. You can't be performing when you're gasping and laughing at the same time.

People · Wisdom · Simple
Talking to a farmer for three hours about rain, patience, and what it means to wait
He had never read a self-help book. He had just paid attention to his land for fifty years. Every sentence he said was worth more than a chapter of anything I'd read.

Faith · Funny · Rain
Getting rained on mid-prayer and laughing through the whole thing
I was on my second raka'a when it started. Lightly at first. Then not lightly at all. I finished soaking wet, couldn't stop laughing. Allah has a sense of humor. I am completely convinced.

06
Lost · Adventure · Best day
I got completely, helplessly lost. It was the best day of my year.
GPS off. Map wrong. Three hours walking in the wrong direction. Found a hidden valley nobody had told me about. Sometimes the trail you weren't supposed to take leads somewhere the correct one never could.

Funny · Animals · Lesson
The goat that ate my notebook, prayer rug, and remaining dignity
I turned around for two minutes. That was all it took. She looked me dead in the eye while chewing my notes. I learned about attachment that day. Also about goats.

Desert · Silence · Night
One night in the desert where I heard nothing for four hours and it was the loudest experience of my life
Real silence — the kind where your own breathing sounds loud — does something to you. It turns the volume up on everything inside that you've been ignoring.
